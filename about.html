About Horizon Event Center

Creating memorable experiences since 2015

Our Story

Horizon Event Center began with a vision: to build a flexible, state-of-the-art space where communities gather, ideas spark, and celebrations shine. Located in the heart of the city, our 25,000 sq ft venue combines modern design with warm hospitality.

Our Mission

  • Deliver flawless events with personalized service
  • Provide inclusive, accessible spaces for all
  • Support local artists, caterers, and businesses
  • Minimize environmental impact through sustainable practices
